House(The Show)

This show is called "House". It's a show with a main character who is a brilliant genius doctor who is forced to work in a hospital. Through the show House, the main character, grows in personality and developes a big connection to some of his colleuges that he works with. Everything slowly unravels as the show goes on, and by the end of the last season. House "dies" and everything collapses. A sad and emotional ending.
